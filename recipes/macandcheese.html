Mac & Cheese

Description

Cook delicious mac & cheese in just 25 minutes with this simple recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil Macaroni
  2. Make a Roux
  3. Add Cheese and Macaroni
